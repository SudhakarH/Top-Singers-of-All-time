DEVI SRI PRASAD

ABOUT

Devi Sri Prasad (born 2 August 1979) is an Indian music composer, lyricist, singer, and performer.[2][3] He is best known for his works in Telugu and Tamil films.[4][5] Prasd is a recipient of nine Filmfare Awards, eight of which are Filmfare Best Music Director South Awards and one Filmfare South Best Background Music, five CineMAA Awards - Best Music Director,[6][7] and one Nandi Award for Best Music Director. Devi also choreographed a song in the 2015 film Kumari 21F.[8][9] As of 2019, he has composed music for more than 100 films in various Indian languages.
