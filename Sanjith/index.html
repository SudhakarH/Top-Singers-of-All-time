SANJITH HEGDE

ABOUT

Sanjith Hegde is an Indian playback singer from Bangalore, Karnataka.[1] Sanjith Hegde has rendered film songs in several Indian languages and has several chart hits to his credit. He has sung songs in Kannada, Tamil, Telugu and Hindi. His rendition of "Gunu Gunuguva" from the film Dalapathi composed by Charan Raj topped the music charts for a year.[2] Sanjith won the Gaana Mirchi Music Award for the Best Upcoming Male Vocalist for the song "Kush Kush" from the movie Chamak composed by Judah Sandhy.
